About Us

An online food ordering system can be defined as software that allows restaurant businesses to accept and manage orders placed over the internet. Online ordering systems generally consists of 2 main components. First is a website or mobile app for hungry customers to view the restaurant's dishes and place an online order. Second is an admin management interface for the restaurants to receive and manage the customer's orders.
